Company and Case Information

RL-Solutions

Case Topic

The world of healthcare is changing dramatically. Patients are no longer satisfied with simply being passive recipients of care. Instead, they now demand to be active participants when it comes to how care is administered to them. Describe what RL, a healthcare software company, can build to help hospitals both solicit and incorporate the voice of the patient into the healthcare delivery model. In addition to conceptualization and design of the product, what is your strategy to bring this product to market?

More about RL-Solutions

RL Solutions designs innovative healthcare software for patient feedback, incident reporting and risk management, infection surveillance, peer review, root cause analysis and claims management. At RL Solutions, nurturing long-lasting relationships with our clients is what we do best. RL Solutions has over 1,500 clients, including healthcare networks, hospitals, long-term care facilities and more. RL Solutions is a global company with offices in Canada, the United States, Australia and the UK. For more information, visit www.rlsolutions.com or follow@rlsolutions on Twitter.

General Electric

Case Topic

The time has come for a next wave of innovation to connect the industrial machines and apply social media principles – connectivity, community and greatly expanded dialogue. “Industrial Internet” is here to enhance business operations ad GE, one of world’s leading providers of solutions in energy, aviation, transportation, finance, health and home is spearheading the effort to forge connections between “things”. Specifically, Industrial Internet breakthroughs will enable GE’s 300,000 employees scattered across 160 countries to help customers anticipate and adapt to future events in real time. How can GE overcome some of the biggest challenges and seize the right opportunities in their path to becoming a leader in Industrial Internet?

More about General Electric Company

General Electric Company (GE) is a diversified technology and financial services company. The products and services of the Company range from aircraft engines, power generation, water processing, and household appliances to medical imaging, business and consumer financing and industrial products. It serves customers in more than 100 countries. GE is one of North America’s largest companies and is ranked among world’s top companies for leaders, and are proud to be one of world’s most admired and innovative companies while maintaining their environmentally sustainable reputation. For more information, visit www.ge.com or follow@generalelectric.
