How to Qualify

1

Deadline: February 28, 2014

1) Organize a team of up to 4 people (students or young professionals) to represent your startup/idea.
2) Create an elevator pitch and complete the application. The elevator pitch will allow you to describe your startup idea and tell us why you deserve to compete at NBTC!

2

Deadline: February 28, 2014

3) The top 30 teams will advance to the preliminary round. From there, they will be divided into two divisions: The Newcomers and The Veterans.

3

Preliminary Round: March 21,2014

4) The top 30 teams will present their 5 minute business pitches to a series of judges from 8AM – 12PM. Your business plan will be evaluated based on technological feasibility, market penetration strategy, organizational structure and financial projections.

4

Final Round: March 21,2014

5) The top 2 teams within each division will be selected to compete in the final round.
