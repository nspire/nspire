What is Growth Hacking?

“Growth Hacking” is a hot term among start-ups since growth is key to success. A growth hacker uses their product knowledge to create high-impact marketing strategies, and ultimately, grow a business. In other words, they are “hacking growth”.

"But I'm not a hacker..."

Many of you are probably growth hacking without realizing it. Do you use social media? Have your own blog? Know how to make videos? These are only a few examples of growth hacking techniques that have been used by many familiar start-ups like Facebook, YouTube, and Dropbox to acquire users or drive engagement.
