Entrepreneurship Challenge

This competition is split into 2 tiers - Newcomer & Veteran. The tier in which you compete in is based on your submitted application.

  1. Prelimary Round

    2. The teams will present their 5 to 10 minute business pitches to a series of judges. Your business plan will be evaluated based on technical feasability, market penetration stragety, organizational structure and financial projections

  2. Final Round

    3. The top 2 teams within each division will be selected to compete in the final round in front of all NBTC delegates!

Competition Application

Application Form

Please fill out the above application to enter yourself into the Entrepeneurship Competition. Listed below, for convenience, are the questions in the application (highly recommended to use word and transfer your answers after):

  1. Name of the company
  2. Name(s) of the team members
  3. Email of main contact
  4. Phone number of main contact
  5. Have you attended NBTC before?
  6. How many individuals are involved in your startup?
  7. Please pitch your startup to us in one sentence
  8. Exntending the last question, tell us why you believe your startup will succeed
  9. Who are your largest competitors in the field?
  10. What stage of the business cycle are you in or working towards?
  11. What makes you stand out from your competition?
  12. How much revenue has your company generated (N/A if this doesn't apply)
  13. What's your most important value that you bring to the table?
  14. Are there any other questions that you have?

If you have any pressing questions, feel free to email the Competitions Lead at victor.denisov@nspire.org.
