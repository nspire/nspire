About NBTC

Over the course of 15 incredible years, The National Business & Technology Conference has grown into Canada’s premiere destination for the next generation of CEOs, founders, and innovators. We are a meeting place for tomorrow’s influencers and today’s changemakers who operate at the intersection of business and technology. Welcome to the stepping stone for the journey to your future.

Competitions

Entrepreneurship Challenge

Our prestigious entrepreneurship competition was established to provide you with unique opportunities to pitch your business and win amazing prizes. Our past winners have gone on to be acquired by Google (BufferBox), win TechCrunch Disrupt (Voltera), and be listed in Forbes 30 Under 30 (Tunezy).

Big Data Competition

The world is filled with data. How do we master this complexity? What are its implications? Our Big Data competition will provide you with both the tools you need to understand this key trend and a platform that will immerse you in the industry. You will gain practical, real-world experience while solving challenging case-based problems.

Health Tech Competition

This unique competition will challenge you to innovate at the intersection of healthcare and technology. You’ll participate in workshops led by industry leaders to give you a feel for the industry. Then, you’ll work off a real-world scenario to innovate and create tangible, feasible ideas for change. Teams will be judged by a panel of industry experts.

Financial Tech Competition

Our case-based FinTech competition is perfect for those interested in analyzing how the finance and technology industries can leverage one another. As a participant, you’ll take part in workshops that will teach you how to effectively tackle your case.
