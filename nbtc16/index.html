A Look Back

Here’s what you need to know: NBTC is not an ordinary conference for ordinary people.

In the past, we’ve hosted the legendary designer Harry Rosen himself, the Presidents of Microsoft Canada and IBM, the Founder of Four Seasons, and senior executive leaders from Facebook, Kobo, Salesforce, and more.

Moreover, our unique atmosphere brings together some of the nation’s brightest minds. In the past, our innovative competitions have sparked new businesses to be acquired by Google, win the prestigious TechCrunch Disrupt competition, and change the face of their industries.
