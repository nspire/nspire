We bring biztech to students.

Nspire has been at the cutting edge of student experiences for nearly 2 decades.

We're challenging perspectives on the influence that business technology can bring to all industries.

We empower 10,000+ students per year through our programs and initiatives.
Across Ontario, we've enabled thousands of university students to work together and build great teams, products, and ideas.

Our portfolio of initiatives across Ontario includes teams of event strategists, marketing gurus, business development ninjas and digital product developers.

We've built relationships with these venerable companies and institutions to establish exciting opportunities for students.

And many more...

Current Initiatives

Here's a look at current and upcoming initiatives. Feel like you could add or improve something to our events? Now's your chance!

Western Our London chapter is focused on delivering the latest develoments in technology to students, through year-round events at the University of Western Ontario (UWO).
Toronto The Nspire Toronto chapter delivers exciting opportunities at all scales for the students of Toronto's many major universities and colleges.
Waterloo Bridging the gap between business and technology for students at the University of Waterloo (UW) and Wilfrid Laurier University (WLU).
Nspire Digital Our Digital team develops all the electronic content you see at our events, on social media, and (of course) this website!

Past and Dormant Initiatives

Over the years, we've had the privilege of building an extensive portfolio of events. These initiatives are currently inactive but may return in the future!

NBTC The National Business and Technology Conference is the foundation upon which Nspire was built: focused on delivering top-notch experiences to 200 of Canada's brightest students every year. We're currently examining new ideas and formats to deliver an even better NBTC in the near future!
Hack the 6ix Nspire's contribution to the illustrious world of MLH hackathons. Continues as an independent annual event. Check them out here!
Nspire TV Our video production team geared toward creating quality content to promote and augment events. Nspire TV has been consolidated into the core of each chapter.
Nteractions An in-house publication focused on business and technology.
