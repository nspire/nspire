WHAT WE'RE UP TO THIS YEAR

A snapshot of new and returning intiatives

WESTERN & WATERLOO

Existing: building upon the success of Ideas Exchange (IDX) on both campuses, continuing to expand in scope and quality.

New: engaging with the Toronto tech scene through inter-campus events and digital ecosystems.

TORONTO

Existing: taking the National Business and Technology Conference (NBTC), Nspire's flagship event, to new heights.

New: creating an experience that positions Nspire as a think tank, creating a two-way conversation on the state of Canadian biztech.

DIGITAL/INTERNAL

Existing: spearheading a culture of excellence in business development, marketing, finance, design, and web development.

New: creating a digital platform to supercharge Nspire's online presence and providing us a base to expand to other campuses in the future.

The quality of our product reflects the quality of our people. We're looking for curious, persistent self-starters to lead the charge and make great things happen.
