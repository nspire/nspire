A non-profit with top talent

We started over 15 years ago as The National Business and Technology Conference. Today, we're Nspire. We're a team of leaders, innovators, and strategists who can engage with an audience of business and engineering students.

Work with teams that build amazing products. Our positions offer an experience unlike side projects or clubs. They build professional-level team skills and agile decision-making abilities.

Unique in every way

The experience you gain at Nspire isn't the franchise of a multi-national student club. We break out of molds, think outside of the box, and encourage you to take your ideas further.

Supercharge your career

When you work with Nspire, you gain access to a network of former members who have years of knowledge, experience and help that they can offer.
