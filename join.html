Who are we?

Nspire Innovation Network is Canada's largest student-run non-profit organization in the business and technology sector!

Nspire is the biztech incubator in Ontario for fast-paced, high impact students. We provide the platform, network, training, and community for you to create something new.

The Nspire team is a diverse powerhouse of students from all manner of universities and disciplines.

We are driven, self-motivated, confident learners willing to push our boundaries and grow.

We are creative, out of the box thinkers who know how to make our vision reality.

We look to the future of business and technology in Canada, and engage with those who will be leading the charge.

Hack the 6ix is the largest hackathon taking place in the heart of downtown Toronto. Every year, developers, designers, and innovators from across the province congregate to brainstorm ideas and build creative projects. Over the course of 36 hours, participants have access to technical and non-technical workshops, fantastic sponsors and an amazing community of tech enthusiasts.

This is a prime example of an idea that started at Nspire and grew wings, now operating as an incorporated non-profit organization!

Ideas Exchange (IDX) is a dynamic way to explore burgeoning technology fields through hands on, skills-oriented, workshop type events. Currently spanning Waterloo, UofT, and Western, events are an intimate way for students to connect with industry leaders in an out of the box creative setting! Whether it's a discussion of the intersection of e-commerce and fashoin, a showcase on the ethics of social media, or a cybersecurity escape room, IDX events leave their attendees excited about the future of biztech and their role in it.
