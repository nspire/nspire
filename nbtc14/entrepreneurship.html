Who is eligible?

The Newcomers

The Newcomers are those who have an idea but have yet to take it to market. They are still under the idea ignition and prototyping stages. The Newcomers have yet to penetrate their space with significant sales, and are currently looking primarily for advice on how to enhance their idea and get ready to execute.

The Veterans

The Veterans are those who have functioning products/services. They have been in market for less than 24 months and have generated significant revenue. They are looking for mentors to help take their business to the next level.

How to Qualify

1

Deadline: February 28, 2014

1) Organize a team of up to 4 people (students or young professionals) to represent your startup/idea.
2) Create a 2 page (maximum) business pitch and complete the application. The business pitch will allow you to describe your startup idea and tell us why you deserve to compete at NBTC!

2

Deadline: February 28, 2014

3) The top 30 teams will advance to the preliminary round. From there, they will be divided into two divisions: The Newcomers and The Veterans.

3

Preliminary Round: March 22,2014

4) The top 30 teams will present their 5 minute business pitches to a series of judges from 8AM – 12PM. Your business plan will be evaluated based on technological feasibility, market penetration strategy, organizational structure and financial projections.

4

Final Round: March 22,2014

5) The top 2 teams within each division will be selected to compete in the final round.
